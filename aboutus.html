WHY CHOOSE US

Welcome to the Shiloh Hills Christian School (SHCS), one of the country’s leading institutions for higher learning and research. At SHCS, we take pride in striving for excellence through the creation, preservation, transfer and application of knowledge. We offer a unique educational experience that prepares the next generation of global citizens to lead and make a difference in the world. At SHCS, every student is immersed in dynamic learning that is fuelled by research-inspired insights and personalized, hands-on experiences that bring learning out of the classroom and into the world.

The school draws a vibrant and diverse community of exceptional students, faculty, alumni, and professionals dedicated to real-world applications of research, scholarship, faith, and service.

At SHCS, you will discover a community rooted in purpose, guided by our founding values, poised to lead in education and research in the 21st century.
